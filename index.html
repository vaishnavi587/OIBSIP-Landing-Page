Our Services

Indonesia: Island Paradise Exploration

Services:

Jakarta: ExploreVista Tours and Travels offers unforgettable adventures in Indonesia. Our expertly curated tours take you to iconic landmarks like Komodo National Park and Bali's rice terraces, and we offer thrilling activities such as snorkeling, diving, and trekking. Tailored itineraries ensure an authentic exploration of Indonesia's natural beauty and cultural heritage.

France: Romantic Retreat

Services:

Paris: Discover the romance and charm of France with ExploreVista Tours and Travels. Our exclusive tours provide access to iconic landmarks like the Eiffel Tower and the Palace of Versailles, along with wine tasting experiences in Bordeaux vineyards and gastronomic tours in Paris. Our tailored packages ensure an enchanting escape in France.

England: Timeless Treasures

Services:

London: Uncover the historic treasures of England with our guided tours of iconic landmarks including Buckingham Palace and the Tower of London and Stone Hedge. Immerse yourself in the world of theatre with tickets and backstage tours in London's West End. Escape to the charming countryside with visits to historic towns like Bath, Oxford, and Stratford-upon-Avon. Enjoy themed experiences such as Harry Potter tours, afternoon tea outings, and pub crawls.

Switzerland: Alpine Adventure

Services:

Bern: Embark on an alpine adventure in Switzerland with our ski and snowboarding packages in renowned resorts like Zermatt and St. Moritz. Experience the breathtaking beauty of the Swiss Alps with scenic train journeys on the Glacier Express or the Bernina Express. Discover the great outdoors with hiking and mountaineering expeditions. Relax and rejuvenate with wellness retreats in spa towns such as Bad Ragaz and Leukerbad.
